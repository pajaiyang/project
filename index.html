The Orgin of D &D

UndergroundSea Dungeons & Dragons group was form when a group of four friends travel to Upper Wisconsin Cabin. They were stuck at the cabin for four days, and they have come up with a strategy to create generic D&D. Over the three months, they have more friends joined the group.

The Core of D&D is a storytelling. Together UndergroundSea tell their story tougher, guiding their heroes through quests for treasure, battles with deadly foes, colt bat, and much more.
